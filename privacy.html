Privacy Policy — Hello Hangul

Last updated: July 2026

Hello Hangul ("the app") is a Korean-language learning app. This page explains what information the app collects and how it's used.

What we collect

Hello Hangul is designed to work entirely on your device. Your lesson progress, XP, streak, hearts, and settings are stored locally in your browser or device storage (localStorage) and are not transmitted to any server or third party by default.

Optional AI tutor feature

If you choose to use the chat tutor feature and it is configured with an AI provider API key, the text you type into the chat is sent to that third-party AI provider (e.g. Groq) to generate a response. No other app data (your progress, name, or device information) is included in that request. If no API key is configured, the tutor uses built-in offline responses only, and nothing is sent anywhere.

Microphone and speech features

Speaking exercises may request access to your device's microphone to let you practice pronunciation, and use your browser's built-in speech recognition. Audio is processed by your browser/device and is not stored or transmitted by this app.

No account, no tracking

Hello Hangul does not require you to create an account,and does not use analytics or tracking services.

Data you can export or delete

You can export a backup of your local progress data from the app's Profile settings at any time, and you can permanently delete all locally stored progress from the same screen.

Children's privacy

Hello Hangul does not knowingly collect personal information from children. Since the app stores data only on-device and does not create accounts, no personal information is transmitted regardless of user age.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this same URL with a new "Last updated" date.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to the developer via the contact details listed on the Google Play Store page for this app.
